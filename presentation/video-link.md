## Contents

The video walks through `presentation/slides.pptx` and includes live demos of:

1. Version A (Durable Functions) - three scenarios driven from PowerShell: A-1 auto-approve, A-2 manager approve, A-3 escalation on timeout.
2. Version B (Logic Apps + Service Bus) - four scenarios driven from Service Bus messages: B-1 auto-approve, B-2 manager approve, B-3 manager reject, B-4 escalation.
3. Comparison across development experience, testability, error handling, human-interaction pattern, observability, and cost, plus my final recommendation.
